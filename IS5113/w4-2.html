2022 NFL Predictions

This visualization of NFL teams predicts regular-season and playoff results by their respective teams' records, and predicts each team's probability of winning a championship.

The advantage is:

  • The past performance and future predictions of each team are very clear.
  • Expressing the probability of success through the depth of color is very intuitive
  • The sorting function can easily filter out the first and last positions of different indicators, which is very useful

The disadvantage is:

  • The numbers are not cool enough to tell the difference in the probability of winning the championship.
